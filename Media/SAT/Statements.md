# Genre & Style
My media product will be a coming of age drama narrative in the form of a printed zine. I've seen these genres produced in media products the follow a similar theme / style to my narrative on a minimal budget, hence I have found it to be the most appropriate path to follow in the production of my zine. These genres often stylistically are a product of their minimal budget, using old hardware with 3CCD sensors that add a level of grain and imperfection to the final image, for my zine I will opt to use 35mm film to recreate this effect through photography to provide authentic imperfection to the final product.
